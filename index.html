About Our Church

Glace Shalom International Church is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to transforming lives through the power of the Gospel.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel, make disciples, and impact nations with the love of Christ.

Our Vision

To raise a generation that reflects God's glory and transforms communities worldwide.

Our Values

Faith, Love, Integrity, Excellence, and Service are the core values that guide our ministry.

Meet Our Pastor

God has blessed us with a visionary leader who is passionate about seeing lives transformed by the Gospel.

Apostle Mathew Mwasiyambiri Jumah

Founder and President of Glace Shalom International Church

Apostle Mathew Mwasiyambiri Jumah is a dynamic preacher, teacher, and visionary leader with a passion for seeing lives transformed by the power of God's Word. With over 15 years of ministry experience, he has dedicated his life to raising a generation that will impact nations for Christ.

Under his leadership, Glace Shalom International Church has grown from a small Bible study group to a thriving international ministry with multiple campuses and outreach programs.
